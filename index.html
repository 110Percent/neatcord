Neatcord

Cool Discord tricks you can show off to your friends.

Using Discord is a lot of fun, but everyone and their dog knows how to use Discord at this point. What if there were more things you could do to Discord to customize your profile, your client and experience even further than you already can?

This guide shows you how to do cool things that can make your Discord experience more enjoyable, or more fun to show to your friends.
